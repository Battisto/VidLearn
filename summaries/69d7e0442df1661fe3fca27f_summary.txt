OVERVIEW
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
This classic fable illustrates the principle of reciprocity through the interaction between a powerful lion and a tiny mouse. After the lion chooses to show mercy to the mouse rather than killing it, the small creature later returns the favor by gnawing through the ropes of a hunter’s net to set the lion free. The narrative emphasizes that no act of kindness is ever wasted, as even the most formidable individuals may one day require assistance from the most humble. Ultimately, the story teaches the moral that small acts of compassion are often rewarded in significant and unexpected ways.
