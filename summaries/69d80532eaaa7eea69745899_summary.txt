This classic fable illustrates the principles of mercy and reciprocity through the interaction between a powerful lion and a tiny mouse. After the lion chooses to spare the mouse’s life, the small creature later returns the favor by gnawing through a trapping net to secure the lion's freedom. The narrative serves as a fundamental lesson in character education, demonstrating that size does not determine one's ability to be of service. Ultimately, the story reinforces the moral that small acts of kindness are powerful investments that often lead to significant future rewards.
