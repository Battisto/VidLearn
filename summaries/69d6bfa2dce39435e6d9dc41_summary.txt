Moral, small acts of kindness will be rewarded greatly. One day, lion was sleeping. The mouse started playing on it. Lion woke up. He caught the mouse and was going to kill. After some days, lion caught in a net. Mouse started chewing the ropes. Soon, the lion was free. He thanked the mouse. The lion let him go. He said: 'I am sorry for what I have done to you. I will never do it again. I am very sorry' The lion then let the mouse go and said he was sorry for his actions. He was very sorry and said that he would never do anything to harm the mouse again.
