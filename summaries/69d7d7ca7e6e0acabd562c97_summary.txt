This classic fable illustrates the principles of mercy and reciprocity through the interaction between a powerful lion and a tiny mouse. After the lion spares the mouse’s life, he later finds himself trapped in a hunter's net, only to be rescued when the mouse returns the favor by gnawing through the restraints. The narrative highlights that no act of compassion is ever wasted, regardless of the stature of the individual involved. Ultimately, the story reinforces the moral that small acts of kindness can lead to significant and life-saving rewards.
